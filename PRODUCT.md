# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, engineering managers, academic peers, and technology enthusiasts seeking to review Vishnu Vardhan M's (Rythamo) specialized work in Generative AI engineering, multi-agent frameworks, and advanced system designs.

## Product Purpose
To present a premium, distinct, and memorable personal portfolio for Vishnu Vardhan M. It aims to prove his computer science rigor, creative engineering capability, and design maturity by displaying real projects and photographs inside a fully functional vintage newspaper aesthetic.

## Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Curious, meticulous, structured, authoritative.
- **Personality**: Classic, intellectual, premium, and distinct.
- **Emotional Goals**: Evokes a sense of nostalgia, tactile craftsmanship, expert capability, and architectural confidence.

## Anti-references
- SaaS Clichés: Saturated tech-purple gradients, generic glassmorphic blocks, and massive soft-glowing drop shadows paired with border lines.
- Endlessly repeating identical card grids with small icons.
- Modern visual noise that lacks historical context or structural rhythm.

## Design Principles
1. **Linotype Meticulousness**: Every element fits into a clear, structured grid. Borders, double hair-lines, and vertical rules define margins and establish visual harmony.
2. **Physical Tangibility**: Emulate natural newspaper materials using organic newsprint background grain, high-contrast monochrome halftone press frames, and slow-stamped circular emblems.
3. **Cognitive Classifieds**: Present technical skills, project bulletins, and contact options as clean classified columns and telegram print outs rather than plain SaaS lists.
4. **Resolute Contrast**: High-contrast ink on newsprint beige provides ultimate textual legibility, proving that accessibility and aesthetic character can coexist perfectly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast levels exceeding WCAG AAA standards (> 7:1 ratio of dark charcoal `#1C1A17` on newsprint background `#FAF6F0`).
- Strict support for `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` to crossfade or instantly snap interactive animations.
- Fully semantic HTML5 sections (`header`, `main`, `footer`, `article`, `dialog`) supporting perfect screen reader accessibility.
